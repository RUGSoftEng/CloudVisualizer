Who are we?

Cloud computing solutions have become increasingly dominant in the software market in the recent years. One of the major reasons driving cloud computing adoption has been perceived to be the leasing of resources (instead of buying them) from cloud providers, in combination with the low prices for these resources achieved by economies of scale on the providersâ€™ side. In other words, cloud consumers, at least when considering the public cloud deployment model, are driven by the promise of lower costs.

This project aims to provide end users, i.e. software developers and DevOps engineers with a set of visualizations that are useful for acquiring an understanding on the projected costs of using one or more public cloud providers during the lifetime of an application
